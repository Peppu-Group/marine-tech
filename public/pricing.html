Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the free trial?

Our 7-day free trial gives you access to all features including proctoring, monitoring, reporting, and analytics. You can create and publish one monitored exam for up to 10 students.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. If you upgrade, your new features will be available immediately. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. For Naira payments, we also accept bank transfers and local payment methods. Enterprise customers may also pay by invoice.

How does the student pricing work?

Our Enterprise pricing is based on the number of students who can take exams concurrently. The more students you need to support simultaneously, the higher the price. Each plan comes with different features tailored to your needs.
